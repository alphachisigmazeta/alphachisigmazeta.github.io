Our Objects:

1) To bind its members with a tie of true and lasting friendship.
2) To strive for the advancement of chemistry both as a science and as a profession.
3) To aid its members by every honorable means in the attainment of their ambitions as
chemists throughout their mortal lives.

We are a professional chemistry fraternity aiming to help the undergraduates and graduates in chemistry at the University of Illinois. We have over 60 active members and have initiated thousands of students and professors, and we are also the largest chapter in the nation!

Members who initiate will make lifelong friends as well have access to awesome resources like professors, study resources, wonderful workshops, and more. If you are interested in joining, we have rush at the beginning of every semester. If you are a professor or company looking to get in contact, feel free to fill out the form in the bottom and we will reach out from there.
